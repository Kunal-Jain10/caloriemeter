If you are like lots of people out there who are struggling to achieve a healthy lifestyle, there are chances that you may have heard a lot of things concerning counting calories. Estimating portion sizes or guesstimating the calorie contents of meals can potentially work if you’ve spent several years measuring, weighing, and tracking your food, but if you’re new to the world of dieting and weight loss, you’re likely to experience greater success by tracking your calorie intake. And, while calorie counting is often panned by diet “gurus” and fasting experts, the simple truth is that it actually works. The only way to know by yourself is - ‘ caloriemeter’.

Caloriemeter is a modern day calorie tracking application, taking you a step forward towards a healthy lifestyle. This application will calculate your daily calorie intake and track the same by the inputs given from your wide database. The user can choose any of the three goals- loose weight, maintain your weight or gain in weight, and caloriemeter will help to achieve the same. You can also look after your daily water intake. Not only your calories intake, this application can also keep a track on the your calories burned. Further, there are various healthy recipes provided to the user which the user can adapt in their lifestyle
